About Henart.Design

Henart Design is a business that focuses on iron crafts, specializing in recycling oil drums into unique and functional works of art. We believe that beauty can be found in everything, including materials that are often considered waste. With a creative touch and the expertise of our craftsmen, used oil drums are transformed into products that not only have high aesthetic value, but also support the principles of sustainability. Located on br bukit Tampaksiring, Gianyar, Bali, Indonesia.

About Our Plan

Our Vision

To be a leader in the sustainable handicraft industry that transforms waste into high-quality works of art, and inspires the world to care more about the environment through innovation and creativity.

Our Mission
